About Us

Our Origin Story

Founded in 2025 by Mukund Bajpai and Sarthak Goyal, our journey began with a vision to transform the e-commerce landscape. Mukund's passion for innovation and Sarthak's strategic mindset created the perfect synergy to launch a platform dedicated to enhancing online shopping experiences. From humble beginnings, their entrepreneurial spirit has driven the company forward.

Their story began with Mukund and Sarthak sharing a deep desire to create an online space that seamlessly combines style, affordability, and high quality. Through tireless efforts and unwavering determination, they embarked on this journey, driven by a mission to cater to the growing tech-savvy audience of India.

Starting with a modest initial capital, the company quickly grew from a small bootstrapped firm to a leading name in the e-commerce sector. What began as a dream to offer stylish products at affordable prices soon evolved into a platform known for its exceptional customer service, innovative products, and positive impact on the community.

Their entrepreneurial journey is marked by perseverance, innovation, and a commitment to learning from every experience. Today, Mukund and Sarthak's vision continues to guide the company, as they strive to deliver the best online shopping experience to customers across the globe.

Business Model

Our platform operates on an agile business model, placing a high priority on understanding consumer needs, desires, and behavior patterns. This approach allows us to quickly adapt and respond in real-time, offering our customers exactly what they want and giving us a significant competitive edge.

Our commitment to continuous innovation is evident in our dynamic product expansion. We offer technological solutions that align with evolving customer demands and consumption patterns, ensuring that we stay ahead of the curve and consistently provide value to our users.

We utilize a multi-channel distribution strategy, actively engaging in online marketplaces and physical storefronts. Our presence on popular e-commerce platforms, combined with our efforts to expand our offline footprint, helps us reach a broader audience. This omnichannel strategy not only contributes to our overall growth and market reach but also ensures accessibility for customers with diverse purchasing preferences.

By staying agile, innovative, and accessible, we aim to create a seamless and enjoyable shopping experience for our customers, driving our success and setting us apart in the competitive e-commerce landscape.
